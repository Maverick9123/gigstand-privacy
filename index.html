Privacy Policy

Effective July 6, 2026

The short version: GigStand does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. It runs entirely on your iPad, offline. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, and no tracking.

Information we collect

None. GigStand does not collect any personal data. There is no account to create, no login, and no email or profile required to use the app. We do not use analytics, advertising, or any tracking technology.

Your songs and content

The songs, chord charts, setlists, backing tracks, and PDF files you create or import stay on your device, in the app's private storage. This content is never uploaded to us or to anyone else, and we have no ability to see it.

Files you import

When you import an audio file (MP3, M4A, or WAV) or a PDF chart, GigStand copies it into its own storage on your device so it works offline. These files are not transmitted anywhere.

Third parties

GigStand contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Because no data leaves your device, none is ever shared with third parties.

Data security

Your data never leaves your iPad, so it is protected by your device's own security. We hold no servers with your information because we never receive any.

Children's privacy

GigStand does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or the app? Visit our support page at www.DreamTeamApps.com/#support.
